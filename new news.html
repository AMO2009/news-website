WELCOME TO THE NEWS

Recently in Turkey and Syria, an earthquake has occurred.
This earthquake has killed over 22,000 people and the number is still rising.
Many people are injured as well as trapped under the rubble.



This earthquake reached a magnitude of 7.8 on the Richter Scale.

The cities affected by earthquake are:

  • Hatay
  • Osmaniye
  • Adiyaman
  • Malatya
  • Sanliurfa
  • Adana
  • Diyarbakir
  • Kilis

Are Turkey and Syria being supported?

Many charities have been created to help support Turkey and Syria in their time of need so even you can help fix the damage.
